Welcome

M&D Event Planners helps you plan and arrange your events your way. Whether you are planning a wedding, a graduation party, or any other ceremony, M&D Event Planners will coordinate the setup and be on site to help the event proceed perfectly to the satisfaction of you and fellow guests.

About Us

My name is Amina Mahamud, proud owner of M&D Event Planners. I grew up planning many events and owning my own business has always been a passion of mine. I would decorate my own room and draft drawings of how I would like to lay out event spaces. With more than 10 years of experience with event planning and wedding decorations, I helped with all of my siblings' weddings and I have honestly never enjoyed doing something as much as I enjoyed designing events. I have moved on to decorating various venues for events such as baby showers, birthdays, & bridal showers.

Event planning is not only a passion of mine, but now my daughter's passion. My daughter has helped me at my events and has fell in love with this career. We decided to name our business M&D for Mother & Daughter. My daughter and I pursue this business with drive and energy to make all events we plan spectacular. We will make sure to give your event the utmost care and work to make it a day you'll never forget!
